# Ebonridge Institute

*A gothic aristocratic adult visual novel built in Ren’Py.*

## Premise

Ebonridge Institute is an elite three-year finishing academy where adults study:

* dominance,
* submission,
* emotional discipline,
* etiquette,
* hierarchy,
* and political power.

Behind its elegant halls and candlelit ceremonies lies a brutal meritocracy where reputation determines survival.

Students rise through the ranks:

* Ivory
* Silver
* Crimson
* Obsidian
* Platinum

—or fall into disgrace.

The academy publicly claims neutrality between Dominants and submissives, men and women alike.

In practice, the system favors a deeply rooted matriarchal hierarchy:

* Female Dominants are institutionally protected.
* Male Dominants are heavily scrutinized.
* Female submissives wield dangerous political influence.
* Male submissives are refined into the social foundation of Ebonridge itself.

Every relationship is strategic.
Every pairing has consequences.

## Features

* 4 playable protagonists:

  * Male Dominant
  * Female Dominant
  * Male Submissive
  * Female Submissive

* Dynamic relationship and pairing systems

* Multiple Year Three standings:

  * Crimson
  * Obsidian
  * Platinum
  * Reassigned/Brat states

* Branching routes influenced by:

  * loyalty,
  * sabotage,
  * composure,
  * prestige,
  * and institutional alignment

* A reactive cast where unplayed protagonists become NPCs with evolving storylines

* Gothic dark-academia aesthetic

* Adult themes including:

  * power exchange,
  * feminization,
  * hierarchy,
  * emotional conditioning,
  * political manipulation,
  * and consensual BDSM dynamics

## Narrative Themes

Ebonridge explores:

* institutional bias,
* emotional control,
* loyalty versus ambition,
* identity reconstruction,
* and whether hierarchy creates order—or simply reproduces itself.
